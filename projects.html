Building a Bicycle

I like to think of myself as a mechanically inclined person so when I was younger I got a used (and abused) single speed bicycle from a donation place. I along with some help from my dad rebuilt the whole thing. That means all new bearings, brakes, a fresh paint job, and new chain and sprockets. This was a cool project for me and it taught me about using tools and how things work mechanically. It also taught me some lessons on lead time as I had to wait for parts to come in the mail.

Skills Learned

  • Mechanical Repair
  • Tool Use
  • Problem Solving
  • Lead Times
